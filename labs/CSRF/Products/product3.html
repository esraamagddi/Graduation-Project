I'm A Photoshopped Girl Living In A Photoshopped World

Allyoucan Pete | 09 March 2023

I don't know what I look like anymore. I never use a mirror, I just look at selfies and use the mirror App on my cell. The mirror App is cool, I always look amazing, and I can change my hair color every day without even having to dye it. There is a wide selection of accessories as well, different hairstyles and any makeup I like. Getting ready takes no time at all, just select the options and go. As well as the App I have virtual reality glasses, on a grey wet day I can pop them on and select a nice sunny scene with luscious green grass and a perfect blue sky. I've saved a fortune since I got the glasses, I no longer need to pay for expensive holidays, I just pop them on and I can be anywhere I want to be. This works really well if I wear them while having a sunbed session, so warm and beautiful and I leave with a tan. The world is such a beautiful place, full of beautiful people and wonderful things. I appear to be losing most of my senses though, I can only see these things, no sound no touching, tasting, smelling or hearing. But that's OK, as long as everything looks perfect. I look perfect all of the time, not a hair out of place. There's no such thing as an ugly duckling with all this fabulous technology at my fingertips. The weird thing though is no-one seems to recognize me when I meet up with friends, and my boyfriend seems to think I should start wearing makeup and brush my hair once in a while. I know what I'm getting him for Christmas, virtual goggles like mine, it's about time he kept up with the trends, and I do rather like a redhead!
